## Statistics

wOBA - Higher is better, aggregates things like Home Runs, Walks, and Hits for overall hitting skill.

WRC+ - Even more distilled then wOBA

xwOBA - expected woba, or expected overall hitting skill, higher is better.

EV - Exit Velocity, the speed the ball comes off the hitters bat.

Chase% - How often you chase a ball outside the zone

PS Score - Takes into account discipline, age, and overall hitting to get the Prospect Score, or hitting potential of the minor league hitter. (0, 1)

WAR - Takes into account Defense, Baserunning, and Hitting at MLB level to get value of player, higher is better.

Any of the columns right of Name_1 are part of MLB data, any left one is part of Minor League data.

## Non-Statcast Statistics


WAR - Takes into account Defense, Baserunning, and Hitting at MLB level to get value of player, higher is better.

K% - The percentage of Place Appearances that end in a strikeout, or three strikes.

BB% - The percentage of Place Appearances that end in a walk, or four balls.

HR - Discrete number of times a batter hit the ball over the fence for a Home Run in a given timeframe.

AVG - The percent of At-Bats that end in a hit in numerical form.

OBP - The percent of Place Appearances that end up with the batter on base in numerical form.

SLG - Single is one point, Double is two, triple is three, and a Home Run is four points, giving a statistics that is higher with more power.

OPS - A very distilled statistic that takes into account all aspects of hitting, giving a higher score for better overall hitting.

BABIP - The amount of balls hit into the field of play that are hits in numerical form.

BB.K - The percent of walks, divided by the percent of strikeouts.

ISO -  SLG minus AVG

wRAA - How many more runs you give your team then the average hitter.

wSB - How many more stolen bases you give our team then the average hitter.
